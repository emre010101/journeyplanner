Welcome to Journey Planner

Welcome to your one-stop solution for all your travel planning needs. Before you get started, here are some simple steps on how to use the platform:

  1. Sign up or log in to the system.
  2. Go to the Planner page. Here, type in your journey's origin and destination along with what you'd like to see along the way in the chat box.
  3. Drag and drop the stop points from the sidebar to the journey bar located below the chat box.
  4. Once you've organized your journey, click on 'Calculate' to view the details of your journey.
  5. After calculating, you can save your journey and share it with others if you wish. You can also view it on Google Maps for a clearer geographical picture.
  6. When you share your journey, you'll be directed to the Community Room, where you'll find your shared journey along with other journeys shared by fellow users.
  7. Don't hesitate to comment, ask questions, or like the journeys. Your feedback and engagement contribute to our growing community.
  8. We hope you have a great experience using our platform. Happy planning!
